Representation

What is representation?

The concept of representation according to Kidd(2015) is concerned with the production of meaning about the ideas that exist in our minds; these ideas are shaped by our experiences in the world, culture.

There are two systems of representation, the first being the conceptual map that we all have; this is how we link certain signs to certain objects or how we link certain signs to have certain meanings, the meaning of course depends on the system of ideas and images that we carry in our heads. It depends on our experiences in the world and our relationship with objects or things in general in the real world.

The second system is the language system, this system depends on the relationship between ideas, things and signs to produce meaning.
